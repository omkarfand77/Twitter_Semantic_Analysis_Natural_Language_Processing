About:

This NLP project focuses on sentiment analysis of tweets, classifying them into four categories:

  • Regular
  • Sarcasm
  • Figurative (both irony and sarcasm)
  • Irony

    • By inputting a tweet, the model predicts the corresponding class,
    enabling nuanced understanding of sentiment in social media discourse.
    Leveraging advanced techniques like tokenization, word embeddings, and transformer architectures,
    the model offers accurate predictions.
